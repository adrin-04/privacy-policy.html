Privacy Policy for Notifi Blocker

Notifi Blocker does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal data under any circumstances.

All notification processing happens locally on your device. The app filters or blocks notifications based solely on the rules you configure. No data is sent to any external servers, and the app does not use analytics or tracking of any kind.

Notifi Blocker does not collect:

  • Device identifiers
  • Personal information
  • Usage statistics
  • Sensitive user or device data

This app operates entirely offline and does not require any network communication to function.
